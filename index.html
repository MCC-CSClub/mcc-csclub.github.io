MIRACOSTA CS CLUB

MEMBERSHIP

Passionate developers who love tech and want to pursue a career in software development.

Starting in Fall 2017, the CS Club was restarted and reinvigorated by a new generation of young computer scientists. We hope to plan some awesome events, including hackathons, tech projects with Raspberry Pi's, this website, and a trip to Google! Please contact us if you'd like to join in on the awesome activities and get to know us more. The membership table below links to the github accounts of our talented young developers.
